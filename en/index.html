Process

How does the cooperation look?

In short: first diagnosis, then work, finally testing.

01

1) Free diagnosis and quote

You describe the issue or what you want to achieve. Together we identify the cause and decide on the scope of work (fixed price for a specific task or project from scratch).

02

2) Secure work and full control

You provide me with temporary access to your system (e.g., by creating a dedicated service account). This allows me to efficiently diagnose the issue and make fixes "under the hood" of your home. We check the results together, and once the work is done, you simply delete my account. You hold the keys to your home – I'm just here to help fix it.

Network stability

Zigbee (Z2M/ZHA) and Wi-Fi diagnostics

I analyze Zigbee network configurations and parameters to pinpoint common stability issues. I help evaluate improvements for settings, network topology, and the radio environment.

  • routing and Link Quality Indicator (LQI) analysis
  • assessment of potential interference and channel conflicts (Zigbee vs. 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi)
  • physical recommendations, e.g., better placement or adding routers
